Welcome to the g2 (gSTAR/swinSTAR) Annual User Feedback Opportunity!

To help us to continue to refine and improve the HPC services that we offer we are seeking feedback on your experience in using the g2 facility at Swinburne (comprising the gSTAR and swinSTAR compute nodes, job queue, lustre storage, etc.).

We estimate that the survey will take less than 10 minutes of your time to complete.

At the end of the survey you have the option of providing your g2 username, thus you may choose to be anonymous or not.
